# Important

* Must Buy Cat6 Crimping Tool, Not Cat5e.

* Includes Stripper And Cutter On Handles.

* Buy Non-Passthrough RJ45 Connector And RJ45 Crimping Tool. Do Not Buy Passthrough Connector And Crimping Tool, They Have Short Circuit Issue.

* A crimping tool with a ratcheting mechanism that ensures the tool fully completes the crimp cycle before releasing. It’s designed to crimp RJ45 (and often RJ11/RJ12) connectors onto cable ends.
  * Ratchet ensures the tool completes a full crimp cycle before opening → more consistent crimps.
  * Non-ratchet can release early → cheaper but more skill needed.

* Crimping Tool Must Have AWG (American Wire Guage) Size 23 Cutting, Stripping And Crimping Capability. For Example: Hikvision 23 AWG CCA Cat6 UTP Ethernet Cable.

* RJ45 Cable Tester Must Have AA/AAA Battery Support, Not Lithium Battery. Lithium Battery Is Not Easily Available In Market And Difficult To Replace.
