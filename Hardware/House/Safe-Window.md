# Safe-Window.md

## Build Instructions

* Set thai glass window in room. Also set mosquito net.

* Set iron frame inside (to protect from rust) and thai glass outside of window. Keep gapes in iron frame to easily open and close thai glass.
  * Setting thai window outside is must for replacing glass and wheel without complete detaching/re-attaching aluminium thai frame.
  * If thai window is set inside, no other way without complete detaching/re-attaching aluminium thai frame.
  * Make aluminium channel longer at outside like door, to completely open thai window.

* Make iron frame as net like shape. Keep frame holes small, so that anyone including children can not enter hand inside of iron frame.
  * Keep clear portion at middle point for opening/closing thai glass and lock.
  * Close that middle point with iron and hinges.
