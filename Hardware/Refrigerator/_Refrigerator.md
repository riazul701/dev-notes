## Notes

## Transportation

* Refrigerator: just handle carefully, keep upright, not laid down, secure load. To let the refrigerant settle properly.

* Before transportation, keep the fridge turned off for 2 days (minimum 1 day). After transportation, keep the fridge turned off for 2 days (minimum 1 day). To let the refrigerant settle properly.

* Need upright transport and rest before powering on — Many mini fridges require that after transport (especially if laid down), you leave them standing upright for several hours (often 12–24h) before plugging in, to let the refrigerant settle properly.
